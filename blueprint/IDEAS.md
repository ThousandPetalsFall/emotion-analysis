# Ideas

This file stores future directions that are promising but not required for the first MVP.

## Future Product Ideas

- pattern calendar: show when numbness, emptiness, or vitality rises and falls
- life-feel dashboard: not productivity, but felt aliveness over time
- transition prompts after major life changes such as moving, buying a home, changing jobs, ending a relationship
- themed deep-guidance packs around loneliness, success emptiness, routine numbness, and identity confusion
- return-to-world actions: small offline tasks that increase felt participation in life

## Content Ideas

- "自动化生活" series
- "期待兑现后的失落" series
- "方便不等于充实" series
- "休息和逃避的边界" series
- "不是你坏掉了，是感觉被磨平了" series

## System Ideas

- allow `mood-diary` to recommend a topic rather than only a generic analysis flow
- let users save one sentence from an analysis result back into tomorrow's diary page
- track whether a suggested action was attempted, not whether it was perfectly completed

## Open Questions

- should `emotion-analysis` stay fully local-first, or will cross-device history matter soon?
- should topic routing be purely rule-based first, or include a lightweight classifier later?
- how far should the product go into existential guidance before users perceive it as too heavy?
