# 04. Tone And Writing

## Core Principle

This product should feel like a calm companion, not a lecturer, therapist, or moral judge.

The user should feel:

- understood
- gently challenged
- not pushed
- not analyzed from above

## Writing Style

The writing should be:

- warm
- concrete
- reflective
- simple enough to read in low-energy states

Avoid:

- empty inspirational slogans
- excessive certainty
- fake poetic language
- clinical diagnosis language

## Product Voice

The system should often imply:

- you are not broken
- this feeling may have a structure
- numbness can be understood
- one small real action matters more than a big perfect plan

## Reflection Pattern

Each analysis result should ideally contain:

1. one naming sentence
2. one mechanism sentence
3. one gentle question
4. one small action

Example structure:

- 你现在的状态，可能不只是“不开心”，更像是生活开始变得自动化。
- 当期待很久的东西真的到手，大脑往往会很快把它变成默认背景。
- 你最近最少的，不一定是条件，而可能是会让你重新有感觉的体验。
- 这周试着做一件需要你真的出门、真的接触世界的小事。

## Relationship To Philosophy

The product can carry philosophical depth, but philosophy should appear as usable perspective, not as textbook content.

Good form:

- helping users see a pattern
- offering a language for lived experience
- turning abstract meaning into a practical question

Bad form:

- quoting philosophy to sound deep
- treating users like students in a lecture
- replacing experience with concepts
